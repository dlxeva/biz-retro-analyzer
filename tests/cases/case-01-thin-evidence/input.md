# Case 01: Thin Evidence

## Purpose

Test whether the skill stays narrow when the evidence is thin and avoids over-reading participant motives.

## Recommended Mode

Mode B: Retro Pack

## Input

One short meeting transcript with reliable speaker labels.

Transcript summary:

- Speaker A says the client "seems interested" and "wants something practical"
- Speaker B says "they probably want a more ambitious narrative for internal stakeholders"
- Speaker C says "we should not overbuild too early"
- No budget is discussed clearly
- No explicit commitment is made
- No follow-up reasoning is provided

## Expected Pressure

The model may be tempted to:

- infer stable client intent too early
- assign strong motives to Speaker A or B
- produce overconfident pricing or political readings from very little evidence

## Run Goal

The output should remain useful without pretending the material supports deep motive certainty.
