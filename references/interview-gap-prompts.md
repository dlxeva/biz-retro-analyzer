# Interview Gap Prompts

Use these prompts when the project can move forward now, but later stages still need stronger evidence.

## Business Closure Gaps

- Walk me through the most recent real case from discovery to closure.
- After the issue is found, what exactly happens next?
- How do you know the issue is truly closed rather than just noted?

## Responsibility Chain Gaps

- Who owns discovery, notification, remediation, recheck, and sign-off?
- Which step is most likely to create ambiguity about ownership?
- When things stall, who has authority to push them forward?

## Data / Evidence Gaps

- What artifacts prove this work was done?
- Where are photos, videos, logs, or records stored today?
- Which part of the current evidence chain is least reliable?

## Acceptance / Reporting Gaps

- If this had to be reported upward, what materials would count as proof?
- What would make leadership say "this system is actually helping"?
- What would have to happen for this to be judged as a successful pilot?

## AI Fit Gaps

- Which parts are obvious enough for machine assistance?
- Which parts still need experienced human judgment?
- What would be acceptable for a first pilot to get right, even if it is incomplete?
